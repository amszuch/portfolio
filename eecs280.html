Project 3: drummvr

Our final project was an open-ended assignment, asking us to create a novel and socially-engaged XR product of our own. I worked in a team of four, and collectively we made drummvr: an interactive VR rhythm game designed as an alternative physical therapy method for individuals with muscular dystrophy. Our product features a variety of tracks, a fully customizable calibration mode to allow for all range of motions, and a complete marketing website to demonstrate key features.
